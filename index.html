...how to make a sandwich?

  1. Get some bread, already cut into slices
  2. Get a knife, some butter, ham, tomato and lettuce
  3. Place 2 slices of bread side by side
  4. Use knife to slather butter unto both bread slices
  5. Place some lettuce on one bread slice
  6. Place some ham on the other slice of bread
  7. Place one or two cut tomato slices on top of the ham
  8. Carefully place bread slices together to make sandwich
